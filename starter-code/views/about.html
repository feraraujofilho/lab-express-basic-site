    • Hip Hop Culture in Berlin - Streetdance at X-Step No fear of Kick Ball Change, Runninng Man and Bart Simpson. We at X-Step in Berlin love the urban dance style and show you how to do the Streetdance! Learn choreographies and techniques and dance to chart hits or timless songs of hip hop, soul pop and rap. Every class starts with a warm-up, where basic Streetdance steps are already integrated. We will learn how to isolate different body parts and of course develop a choreography. Having lots of fun you will not only get your body fit but also improve your sense of rhythm and dance skills. Come and dance with us!
